Solar System Planets 🪐

Mercury

The smallest planet and closest to the Sun.

Special: Extreme temperature changes.

Venus

The hottest planet with thick CO₂ atmosphere.

Special: Rotates opposite to most planets.

Earth

The only known planet that supports life.

Special: 70% surface covered with water.

Mars

The red planet explored by rovers.

Special: Home to the largest volcano in the solar system.

Jupiter

The largest planet in the solar system.

Saturn

Famous for its beautiful ring system.

Uranus

An ice giant that rotates on its side.

Neptune

Famous Moons 🌙

Earth's Moon

The natural satellite of Earth.

Special: Controls tides and stabilizes Earth's rotation.

Europa

Moon of Jupiter covered with ice.

Special: May contain a hidden ocean.

Titan

The largest moon of Saturn.

Special: Has lakes of liquid methane.

Ganymede

The largest moon in the solar system.

Special: Larger than the planet Mercury.

Asteroids ☄

Ceres

The largest object in the asteroid belt.

Special: Classified as a dwarf planet.

Vesta

One of the biggest asteroids.

Special: Has a giant crater at its south pole.

Eros

A near-Earth asteroid.

Special: One of the first asteroids orbited by a spacecraft.

Psyche

A metal-rich asteroid.

Special: May contain large amounts of iron and nickel.
